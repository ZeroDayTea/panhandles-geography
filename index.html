Panhandles Geography

Directions:

Select your preferred gamemode and click on the country indicated by the target near your cursor

World Geography includes all of the countries of the world while Panhandles Geography tasks you to identify the major panhandles (salients) of our world

Use [Alt][Scroll] to zoom in and out of the map (this is helpful for smaller nations such as those in the Caribbean)

Click and drag to pan across the map

If you guess incorrectly 3 times in a row the correct country or panhandle will begin blinking. If it is small enough, however, you will still need to know the region of the world it is in
